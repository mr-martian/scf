The Swarthmore Christian Fellowship is a non-denominational group of Swarthmore Students that are dedicated to serving God and Christ on this campus. We are affiliated with Inter-Varsity Christian Fellowships.

If SCf is new to you, here is a more complete introduction to who we are. Please tell us that you visited by filling out this simple form.

Our Vision for Fall '97

That we would not love with words or tongue but in actions and in truth. (inspired by 1 John 3:16)

Here is what we looked like at the Intervarsity summer Chapter Camp in '96.

We also take weekend Weekend Retreats twice a year.

As Christians we believe that The Bible is the inspired word of God and we try to follow its teaching and truth in our lives.
