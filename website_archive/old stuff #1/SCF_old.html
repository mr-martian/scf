The Swarthmore Christian Fellowship is a non-denominational group of Swarthmore Students that are dedicated to serving God and Christ on this campus. We are affiliated with Inter- Varsity Christian Fellowships and have an active membership of about 60 (our mailing list is around 120).
If you would like to read the official blurb about who we are then go here. Please tell us that you visited by filling out this simple form.
