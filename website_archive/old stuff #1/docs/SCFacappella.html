SCF's Links to Christian A Cappella Groups

	SCF has had the pleasure of hearing Swarthmore's own a cappella
	group Psalters on a number of occasions.  Here is a big list of
	Christian a cappella groups from a number of schools and how 
	you can get in touch with them.

 
Have we missed a group?  If you know of a collegiate Christian a cappella
group which isn't correctly listed here, or if some group has a new homepage,
send email to Jai Shin about it.


To reach lots and lots of these groups all at once,
send email to the xian-acappella@mit.edu mailing list.


To communicate with the administrators of the xian-acappella@mit.edu mailing list,
send email to xian-acappella-request@mit.edu.
