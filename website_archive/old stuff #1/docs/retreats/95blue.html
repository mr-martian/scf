SCF 1995 Winter Retreat

	The winter retreat was held (as always) in conjunction with Bryn Mawr
and Haverford Christian Fellowships on January 27-28, at the Blue Chuch, 
right here in  Springfield, Pennsylvania.  The speakers for the weekend were
Dick and Mardi Keyes from the Boston area L'Abri.


	The weekend was a mini-conference tackling the complex questions
of a tough topic.  Talks discussed such topics as:
  • how important is gender identity?
  • what ought to be the hub of both men's and women's identity?
  • what is "male" idendity? "female" identity? are these of God?
  • what does God sya about gender roles at work and at home?
  • are Christianity and feminism compatible? how?
  • is Paul's view of marriage misogynist?
