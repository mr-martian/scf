Why Evangelism?


SCF 1996 Winter Retreat

	From Friday, February 1, to Saturday, February 2, the Swarthmore
Christian Fellowship held its annual winter retreat at the Blue Church together
with Bryn Mawr and Haverford Christian Fellowships.  This year's topic was
Why Evangelism? and our speaker was Greg Baker, the Intervarsity Staff Worker
for University of Delaware Christian Fellowship.


	Talks focussed on broader topics of:
  • The Whole World Will Know There is a God in Israel
  • He Ran Out to the End of the Road to Meet Him
  • The Mind of Christ
  • God-centered Evangelism
