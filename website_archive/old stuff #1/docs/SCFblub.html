What We Are

The Swarthmore Christian Fellowsh (SCF) is a non-denominational community of students who follow Jesus Christ as their Lord and Savior. We Believe that Jesus is the son of God and in the Divine inspiration and authority of the Bible; we seek to share the Good News of Jesus Christ with the campus. We are an officially chartered student organization of Swarthmore College. We have placed our official charter online so that you can read it if you wish.

The entire Fellowship meets in Bond on Friday evenings at 7:30 p.m. All are welcome to attend, regardless of their own religious beliefs. In addition to singing and prayer, Fellowship meetings sometimes feature guest speakers who discuss various issues relating to the Christian faith. The meetings can also follow a more informal format, including sharing experiences, discussing issues, and doing fun activites. Other all-fellowship activities include retreats each semester, and Chapter Camp after exams every year.

Smaller groups gather by dorm during the week, taking the time out from academics to study the Bible. If you are interested, a schedule of all the small groups on campus is available.

SCF also schedules daily prayer meetings which meet in the SCF room in the basement of Bond, Monday through Friday at 8:00 am and again at 5:00 p.m.

Students who want to find out more about Jesus Christ may want to join an Investigative Bible Study (IBS) to examine and discuss the Scriptures. In order to get involved in an IBS, please contact our Evangelism Coordinator, Michelle Park .

The Fellowship sponsors several all-college events throughout the year, including the Ice Cream Social and the Easter Sunrise Service, and hosts several all-campus speakers.

SCF is affiliated with Inter-Varsity Christian Fellowship, a national organization of college, university, and nursing school students.
