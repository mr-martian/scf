Who We Are

Swarthmore Christian Fellowship (SCF) is a multi-ethnic, student-led ministry that seeks to love and glorify Jesus Christ through vibrant community! SCF meets throughout the week in a variety of settings, including bible studies and prayer meetings. We welcome everyone, regardless of background or belief. There are currently 45-50 active SCF members and around 150 Swarthmore students on our mailing list.

Swarthmore Christian Fellowship (SCF) is a chapter of the non-denominational Christian ministry, InterVarsity. To learn more about InterVarsity, check out their website. More information about SCF in particular can be found in our charter.
