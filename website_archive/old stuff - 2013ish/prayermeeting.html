Prayer Gathering

Do not be anxious about anything, but in everything, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God. And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus. (Philippians 4:6-7)

This academic year, prayer gathering will take place in Kohlberg 116 from 7:00-9:00 PM.

Once a week, all of SCF gathers for a time of prayer. Through prayer, we seek to develop a closer relationship with God and worship Him. You can contact our prayer coordinator Joe Landis ('16) at jlandis1@swarthmore.edu for more information.
