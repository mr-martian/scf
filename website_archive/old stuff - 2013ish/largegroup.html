Large Group

Large group is a time for everyone to come together at the end of those long Swarthmore weeks. A typical large group will involve listening to a talk given by an outside speaker and worshiping corporately in song. These gatherings provide a restful opportunity to grown in our faith, build community, and glorify God together.

Large groups generally meet in Science Center 181 on Fridays from 7:00-8:30 PM. Check out the schedule below to see if there are any special large group events or speakers coming in the near future. If you have questions about large group, contact our large group coordinator Medgine Elie at melie1@swarthmore.edu.

Also, don't forget SCF dinner! Many of us will meet for dinner before Large Group in Sharples Room L4 from 5:30-6:45 PM.
