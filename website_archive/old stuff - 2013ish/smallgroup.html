Small Group

Small groups gather once a week and provide an opportunity to get to know God and other SCFers more personally. Most meet for about an hour and a half and usually include times for prayer, studying the Bible, and catching up. All small groups are led by one or two of your fellow students, and everyone is welcome to attend. A complete listing of the groups and their meeting information can be found below.

Each semester, all small groups study a book of the Bible. Small group leaders will provide copies of the text for you, so there is no need to bring a Bible yourself. We hope you will attend and use your small group as an opportunity to delve deeply into questions about Christianity and become more familiar with the Word.
