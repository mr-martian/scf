SWARTHMORE CHRISTIAN FELLOWSHIP

A chapter of InterVarsity Christian Fellowship USA

We are a multi-ethnic, cross-denominational, student-led ministry that seeks to love and glorify Jesus Christ through vibrant community. We meets throughout the week in a variety of settings, including small group bible studies, large group fellowship, and prayer gatherings, and service events. We welcome everyone to join the fellowship regardless of background or belief. We are a chapter of the non-denominational InterVarsity Christian Fellowship ministry.
